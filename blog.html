March 3, 2021

This week's post will cover an interesting attack that uses the lidar sensors in robot vacuum cleaners to eavesdrop on sensitive audio. This is one specific attack that lies under a more general class of attacks that are concerned with collecting some sort of personal information through vulnerabilities within smart devices. This side channel attack was able to collect information playing from someone's TV and computer, both of which can lead to serious security issues. Click to read more.
